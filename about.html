Our Culinary Journey

From a small family kitchen to your favorite dining experience

The TastyBites Story

Founded in 2005 by Chef Marco Santini, TastyBites began as a dream to bring authentic Italian flavors with a modern twist to food lovers everywhere. What started as a 12-seat trattoria in Little Italy has grown into an award-winning culinary destination.

2005

Chef Marco opens the first TastyBites with just four tables and a wood-fired oven imported from Naples.

2008

Featured in "Best New Restaurants" by Food & Wine Magazine, putting us on the culinary map.

2012

Expanded to our current location, featuring an open kitchen and garden patio seating.

2016

Received the prestigious "Golden Fork" award for culinary excellence.

2020

Launched our farm-to-table initiative, partnering with local organic farms.

2025

Celebrated our 18th anniversary with a complete kitchen renovation and new seasonal menu.
